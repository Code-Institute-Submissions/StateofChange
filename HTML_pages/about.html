About me

Hello there! I'm Rene, a passionate filmmaker and podcast creator based in the vibrant city of Berlin. At the age of 38, I have been on an incredible journey that led me to explore my deep love for storytelling through visuals and audio. For over 15 years, I worked as a freelancer, honing my skills as a professional photographer and director. During this time, I had the privilege of working on various projects, collaborating with talented individuals, and learning from diverse cultures worldwide. These experiences enriched my storytelling abilities and further fueled my passion for visual storytelling. Thank you for being a part of this remarkable adventure.
